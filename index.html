Our Story

Doner Delight was born from a passion for authentic Mediterranean flavors and a desire to bring the perfect kebab to the streets of Northern Ireland.

Founded in 2018 by the Savage family, who brought their secret recipes from the Shankhill, we've quickly become a favorite spot for kebab lovers across Belfast, Lisburn, and Moira.

Our commitment to quality ingredients, expert preparation, and friendly service has earned us a loyal following and numerous awards in the local food scene.

Every dish we serve is prepared with care, combining traditional methods with innovative touches to create a truly unforgettable dining experience.
